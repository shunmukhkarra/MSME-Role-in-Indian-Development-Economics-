# Role of MSMEs in India's Economic Development

This project analyzes the contribution of Micro, Small, and Medium Enterprises (MSMEs) to India's economy and the major challenges affecting their growth, including credit constraints, competition from MNCs, infrastructure issues, raw material shortages, and technology gaps.

## Objectives
- Study the economic importance of MSMEs in India.
- Identify key challenges faced by the sector.
- Analyze government initiatives and policy recommendations.

## Methodology
- Descriptive and analytical research using secondary data.
- Data sources: Ministry of MSME, RBI, SIDBI, World Bank, and FICCI reports.

## Key Findings
- MSMEs contribute significantly to India's GDP, employment, and exports.
- Limited access to credit and low technology adoption remain major barriers to growth.
